# **Project Objective**

The Customer Buying Behaviour Analysis project aimed to analyze customer purchasing patterns and spending behaviour using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard was designed to uncover insights related to customer demographics, purchasing trends, product categories, and seasonal sales performance.

The project focused on transforming raw sales data into interactive visual reports and dashboards to support data-driven business decisions.

---

# **Dataset**

- Customer purchase and transaction data
- Demographic and product category information
- Seasonal and location-based sales records

---

# **Key Business Questions**

- Which gender contributes the highest purchase amount?
- Which product categories generate the highest average sales?
- Which locations have the highest purchase amounts?
- What are the most purchased items?
- How does customer age impact purchasing behaviour?
- Which season records the highest average purchase amount?

---

# **Process & Methodology**

### **Data Cleaning**
- Removed duplicates and handled missing values
- Standardized data formats and categories
- Prepared datasets for analysis

### **Data Analysis**
- Used Pivot Tables and Excel formulas for analysis
- Analyzed customer demographics and purchasing trends
- Compared sales across categories, locations, and seasons

### **Dashboard Development**
- Built interactive dashboards using charts and slicers
- Created KPI visuals for customer insights
- Designed a clean and business-focused layout

---

# **Dashboard Insights**

- Male customers contributed the highest purchase amount
- Footwear recorded the highest average purchase category
- Montana generated the highest purchase amount among locations
- T-shirts, Jewelry, and Pants were among the top purchased items
- Fall season showed the highest average purchase amount
